About Me

I love coffee

But unfortunately my gut cannot currently handle it.

Breakfast is the best dish

Though with a low-FODMAP diet, I start my day with rolled oats, peanut butter, cocoa and blueberries.

I like post-metal

Apparently, because I love Deafheaven, which is a post-metal genre band.

I really enjoy working with code. As much as I despise its frustrating qualities, I also live for the feeling of reward when I unlock the secret of a computer. My vision is to become a full-stack web developer, and eventually a data scientist. I will eventually contribute to large scale solutions that effect millions of people.

I grew up with a family of restaurant workers. I didn't learn the basics of IT until my mid twenties. Since then, I've commanded markets of computer sales, troubleshot Windows and Mac computers, provided Support for a Salesforce application and developed webpages for a Continuing Medical Education (CME) company.
